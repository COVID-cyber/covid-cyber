Be safe online during COVID-19 Pandemic
About the Project

Services

This project is aimed at keeping a check on cybercrime, and cybercriminals exploiting the COVID-19 virus pandemic

We need your support!

With the COVID19 Pandemic, we are working away in the background where there is a massive range of cyber threats and also logistical challenges in our path. Healthcare (and other sensitive/critical services) is not somewhere where we can rock up and start jumping on systems and networks, even just organisationally. From a common point of view, the challenges are not small. We want to make sure that as a group, we can help no matter where we are.

A Force for Good

Cyber Peace Volunteers come from varied backgrounds and cultures. Through their knowledge of Cyber, Technology, their skills, and their know-how, they contribute to programs and projects which aim at improving the lives of communities in need. They work for humanity!

In this challenging time, we need your help and support!

If you are a cyber expert, humanitarian, social entrepreneur, techie, or student with rigor to solve challenges, please fill up the form
